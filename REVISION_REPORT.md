## Ablation Configurations

All ablations use the chronological target-week split, validation-selected thresholds, raw-score PR-AUC/ROC-AUC, and a 300,000-row stratified training sample:

- Historical demand only
- Historical + calendar
- Historical + calendar + weather
- Historical + calendar + weather + OSM
- Historical + calendar + weather + PLUTO
- Full context

Feature-set composition was checked against `data/processed/_model_summaries/inspect_final_dataset_feature_sets.json`. The history-only row includes complaint category, borough, current `complaint_count`, lag/difference/rolling/ratio demand features, and history-availability flags. Complaint category and current demand are retained in every ablation. Calendar and weather are cumulative additions; OSM and PLUTO are alternative additions after weather; full context includes both OSM and PLUTO.
