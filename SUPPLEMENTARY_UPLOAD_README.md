# Supplementary Upload Package

Upload `paper_springer/supplementary_SIMC.pdf` together with the main SIMC manuscript PDF.

The PDF is reviewer-facing and contains:

- held-out-2025 T2 performance by train-derived historical-volume decile;
- deterministic complaint-type to nine-category mapping and the composition of `other`;
- same-target T2 metrics for Poisson GLM, Poisson GLM plus NTA fixed effects, HGB Poisson, hurdle HGB, and the selected LightGBM;
- cluster-bootstrap baseline intervals, five-seed stability, and rolling-origin results;
- calibration diagnostics and labelled T0/T1/T2/T3 sensitivity evidence; and
- the Negative Binomial tooling limitation.

The companion CSVs in `data/processed/model_results/major_revision/supplementary/` are reproducibility artifacts, not required submission uploads unless the venue requests machine-readable tables.
